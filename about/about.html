About Me

Hi, I'm Jeremiah. Welcome to Badger Studios. I am currently an undergraduate student athlete trying to obtain my degree in Studio Art at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. In 2018, I graduated from First Flight High School as a 2x State Champion in North Carolina for wrestling and was recruited by UNC to wrestle Division 1. I am also a self publishing comic book artist unde my label Badger Studios and as of now have only publish on comic book titled, "Rue." I am currently creating my very first graphic novel called "Tzaria" and have created this website to give you behind the scenes looks at the project as well as share my process.


My mission for Badger Studios is to teach you how to create and self publish your own comic books as well as share my own work. I hope you enjoy what I am doing and learn something along the way.
